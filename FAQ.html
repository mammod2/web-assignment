How are the kits shipped?
Our ramen kits are shipped fresh in a cardboard box with a layer of insulated liners, cool shield bubble mailers, and ice packs to ensure products arrive in the highest quality. Because it is shipped with ice packs/dry ice, your ramen kit contents may freeze in transit.
